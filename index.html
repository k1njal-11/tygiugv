01 — What is a career simulation?

Okay. You're five.
Here's the deal.

You know how you play "office-office" with your friends — someone's the boss, someone sells the toys?

A career simulation is that game, made to feel real. Real projects, real deadlines, a real "boss", a teammate who disagrees with you. But because it's a game, you can't actually lose — mess up the pitch, nobody gets fired. You just try again, better.

Do that enough times, and when the real world shows up… it feels like a game you've already played. That's the edge.

03 — Why it matters

Grades open doors.
Soft skills keep them open.

AI can write the code and crunch the numbers. It can't read a room, win trust, or rally a team. In every field, the people who rise are the ones machines can't imitate.

  • Negotiate internships, salaries & opportunities
  • Stay calm and useful when plans fall apart
